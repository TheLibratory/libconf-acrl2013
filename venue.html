Workshop Location

The conference will be held in the Perry-Castañeda Library (PCL) in the University Federal Credit Union Student Learning Commons (PCL 2.500).

Perry-Castañeda Library Map

The Perry-Castañeda Library is located at 21st and Speedway. For those driving and needing to park, PCL is most conveniently served by the Brazos Garage where a full day parking ticket will run about $18.
